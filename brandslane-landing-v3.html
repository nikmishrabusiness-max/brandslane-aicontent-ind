Common Questions
You're Skeptical. Good.

These are the questions every e-commerce founder asks before working with us.

Will customers notice the content is AI-generated?
No. We build custom AI creator models that produce photorealistic, UGC-style content indistinguishable from human-shot videos. Your audience sees authentic product content — not the production method behind it.
How does AI content perform compared to real UGC?
AI content performs at equal or better levels — but the real advantage is volume. When you can test significantly more variations, you find winners faster and refresh fatigued creative sooner. The compounding effect of higher testing velocity is what drives the performance gap.
What if I already have UGC creators I work with?
Keep them. Most brands use us to supplement their existing pipeline — your top creators handle flagship content while we fill the testing queue with volume. More tests, faster winners, best people focused on what they do best.
Can't I just use AI tools like Higgsfield.ai or HeyGen myself?
You could try. Most brands do — and most find the output doesn't match what they saw in the promotional videos. Tools like Higgsfield.ai or HeyGen require significant expertise in prompting, model training, and post-production to get ad-account-ready results. We've built that expertise so you don't have to.
What verticals do you work with?
E-commerce — skincare, beauty, fashion, supplements, wellness, accessories, and home goods. If you're selling a physical product online and need more creative volume than your current setup can produce, we're built for you.
Do I own the content you produce?
Yes. Every asset is yours — ads, organic, website, email, wherever. Full ownership, no licensing restrictions. If we stop working together, you keep everything.
